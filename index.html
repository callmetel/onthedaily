Vision Statement

AetnaCare isn't merely a unique twist on the traditional care model. It's transforming the definition of care, and what it should achieve.

Everyone, from high- to low-risk members, has the ability to thrive. And we can empower them to take control of their health. Our growing partnerships will help us create an optimal ecosystem within our members' communities. And in it, they'll have the why they need to become and stay active in a healthier life.

Health benefits and health insurance plans contain exclusions and limitations.
